Toronto

Toronto is the biggest city in Canada. It is located in the state Ontario. Toronto has almost 3 milion inhabitants.

The city is best known for the CN Tower. This tower is 553 meters high. Until September 13, 2007, this was the world's tallest free-standing structure on land

Toronto has two major football clubs, namely Toronto FC which plays in the MLS and York United FC which plays in the Canadian Premier League. A well-known basketball club is the Toronto Raptors. The biggest ice hockey club is the Toronto Maple Leafs.

Barcelona

Barcelona is one of the biggest cities on Spain, only the capital Madrid has more inhabitants.

Barcelona is the capital from the area Catalonia. The city has more than 1.5 million inhabitants.

Located on the Mediterranean Sea, on the Costa del Maresme, with the Costa Brava to the north and the Costa del Garraf to the south, Barcelona has an important port.

Spain have a lot of big footballclubs, one of them is located in Barcelona, called 'FC Barcelona'. This club has a big history, they have won everything that they could win.

Rotterdam

Rotterdam, a port city located in the west of the Netherlands, is the second biggest city in The Netherlands.

The city is located on the Nieuwe Maas, one of the rivers in the delta formed by the Rhine and the Meuse.

After the historic city center was largely destroyed by a German war bombing in 1940, Rotterdam has become a cradle for innovative architecture, including the Erasmus Bridge, the Cube Houses and a large number of skyscrapers.
